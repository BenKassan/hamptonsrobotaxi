Powered by Tesla

We provide a Tesla equipped with Full Self-Driving technology and a professional driver to our partner hotels. Guests get complimentary rides anywhere in East Hampton — the beach, dinner, the train station. The hotel pays nothing. We handle the car, the driver, and the insurance.

For Guests

Complimentary rides in a Tesla. Safe, seamless, and available whenever you need one. Just ask the front desk.

For Hotels

For the Community

Fewer impaired drivers. More accessible transportation. A safer East Hampton for everyone.

Self-Driving Technology.
Human Safety.

Our Teslas use Full Self-Driving (Supervised) — the most advanced driver-assistance system on the road. A trained professional driver is always behind the wheel, supervising every ride. Guests can watch the car navigate in real time on the center screen, or simply enjoy the ride.
