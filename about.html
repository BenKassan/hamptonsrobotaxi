How It Works

Three simple steps

1. We Partner With a Hotel

We place a Tesla at your property with a professional driver. Everything is covered — the car, the insurance, the operations.

2. Guests Request a Ride

Through the front desk or a simple text message. No app required. No complicated booking process.

3. We Handle Everything

The car, the driver, the insurance, the technology. The hotel just enjoys happy guests.

The Technology

Advanced driver-assistance technology

Tesla's Full Self-Driving system uses cameras and AI to navigate roads. A professional driver is always present and in control. The system assists with steering, acceleration, and braking while the driver supervises.
